 WHO WE ARE

Technologies & Solutions Energy Services Company Ltd. Is a supplier of diversified solutions, technologies and services to the oil, gas, water and petrochemical industries in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Energy Solutions and Technologies Ltd. For Petroleum, Gas and Chemical Services was established in 2022 AD to keep pace with the expected huge growth in the industrial sectors, especially the oil, gas and chemical sectors, and the need to secure production safety by finding advanced and appropriate solutions for cleaning, corrosion reduction, repair, rehabilitation, insulation work and rust inspection . The company ranks first among other competitors as a supplier of high quality chemicals and a specialized contractor for the most important chemical and petroleum services in the region. The company also provides a range of important services in various sectors, most notably the selection of solutions for cleaning soil and water from oil and corrosion, pipeline rehabilitation, inspection, and oil services in various sections: well testing, backside pump and wireline.
The Energy Services Solutions and Technologies team includes the most skilled and knowledgeable expertise in most practical aspects of this field, making it distinct from other competing companies.
